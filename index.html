Welcome to my Mexican Vacation

In October of 2016 my boyfriend and I decided to visit the beautiful country of Mexico. For our first tour we decided to explore Isla de Mujeres which is "Island of the Women". It's a small island about 2 miles long. This island is a very special one since it's believed that it was sacred to the Maya Goddess of childbirth and medicine, Ixchel.

For our second tour we decided to explore the ancient city of Chichén Itzá (which means "the mouth of the well of the Itzas") which was truly an awe-inspiring archaelogical site you can visit. Our tour guides took us on such a fascinating guide filled with Mayan culture and history.

Our third & final tour was Tulum. The city of Tulum was previously known as Zama, meaning the city of Dawn since it faces the sunrise. Tulúm also means fence, or wall in Mayan. The walls that surrounded this site was for the purpose of defense against any invasions. It is also believed to have been a site of worship for the Descending God.

I invite you to explore my site to get a little jist of just how beautiful and rich in culture this beatiful city is. I definitely recommend anyone going to Mexico to definitely do the tours since it's defintely worth it. The food was incredible and the people truly amazing. Thank you Mexico for my wonderful vacation experience. I defintely plan to return.
